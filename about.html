I moved to Manhattan and never left. Gramercy. Kips Bay. Murray Hill. Thirty years of paying rent, watching neighborhoods shift, and learning the difference between a good building and a great deal.

This city has a way of sorting people fast. You either learn how it works or you get worked. I got here early enough to watch the neighborhoods before they got expensive, and I stayed long enough to understand why the ones that held their value did.

"I don't perform. I don't chase.
I deal in reality."

I work with landlords who need their units filled, renters who need real representation, buyers who want an honest read before they sign anything, and sellers who need clean execution. No conflicts, no divided loyalties — just straight talk and a market you can actually navigate.

The people I work with best aren't looking for a salesperson. They're looking for someone who asks the right questions before the paperwork starts. That's the conversation I want to have.

I'm licensed with NY Living Solutions | Keller Williams NYC. I don't run a team. I work directly with every client. If you're in, I'm in. If it stops making sense for you, we stop. That's not a line. That's how I work.
