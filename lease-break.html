The Numbers

Our Fee vs.
Your Liability.

Under NY Real Property Law § 227-e, your landlord must make reasonable efforts to re-rent — but you remain liable for every month of remaining rent until they do. On a $4,500 apartment, that's $9,000–$18,000+ in unpaid rent exposure over 2–4 months. We find the replacement tenant. Your liability stops the day they sign. Our 7% fee is the cost of making that happen cleanly.
