About This Property

A Solid Lot and a Real Opportunity in Queens Village

This 1950 Cape Cod sits on a full 40×100 lot in Queens Village — a neighborhood where detached single-family homes in this zip code are trading at a median of $789,000. At 1,304 square feet across two stories, the bones are there. The buyer who wins here is the one who can see past the current condition and run the numbers.

Sold as-is. The seller is disclosing known issues upfront — water damage, mold in the basement, and electrical work needed. Nothing here is a surprise. Everything is priced accordingly. For a renovation buyer or investor, the carrying costs are manageable: $5,767 in annual taxes is low for Queens, and the lot size gives you genuine outdoor space on a residential block.

Queens Village sits near the Nassau County border, with LIRR access at the Laurelton and Locust Manor stations. It's a stable, owner-occupied neighborhood with a strong buyer pool — which is exactly why this will move quickly for the right person.
